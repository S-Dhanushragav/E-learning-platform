Critical Thinking and Problem Solving

Course Description

Critical Thinking and Problem Solving course equips learners with essential skills for analyzing, evaluating, and solving complex problems effectively. Participants will develop critical thinking strategies, including logical reasoning, argument analysis, and decision-making techniques. The course also covers problem-solving methodologies such as root cause analysis, brainstorming, and decision trees, empowering learners to tackle challenges with confidence.
